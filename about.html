About Us

At Toronto Typewriters, we're more than just a place to find vintage typewriters. We embody the spirit of Marshall McLuhan's quote, "The medium is the message." Our story is one of timeless communication, where every keystroke carries the weight of history and craftsmanship.

In a digital age, we celebrate the tangible, the tactile, and the typewritten word. We believe that each typewriter has its unique voice, its own story to tell. We're dedicated to preserving this medium and sharing its magic with the world.

Our journey is one of nostalgia, creativity, and the love of the written word. Join us in discovering the power of typewriters and how they continue to shape the message in a rapidly changing world. Welcome to Toronto Typewriters, where the medium truly is the message.

Join us on our journey and help bring back the Art of Communication!
